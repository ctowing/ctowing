Services

Whether your problem is large or small, Chatham Towing is able to help with all of your towing needs.

Our Services include, but are not limited to:

Tire Services

Is your tire soft or completely flat? We may be able to change your tire if you have a spare, or we can tow you to a garage so they can assist you.

Boosting & Battery Services

Are you having trouble starting your car? We may be able to give you a boost, or if needed, we can tow you to a garage so they can assist you.

Lock-Out Assistance

Locked your keys in your vehicle? We have specialized tools to unlock your vehicle so you can access your keys and be on your way.

Out of Fuel

Ran out of fuel before you could get to a station? We can deliver fuel to you.

Light, Medium & Heavy Duty Towing

Whether your vehicle is a motorcycle, car, pick-up truck, or transport truck, and whatever the situation, Chatham Towing can help.

Short & Long Distance Towing

Whether you need your car towed within Chatham or whether you need to be towed to a different city – we’re here to help!

Services Canada & United States

If you need your vehicle towed within Canada, we can do that. If you need your vehicle towed from Canada to the United States, we can do that too. Or, if you need your vehicle towed from the United States to Canada, we can also do that!

Vehicle Recovery

Whether your vehicle is a motorcycle, car, pick-up truck, or transport truck, and whatever the situation, Chatham Towing can help.

Storage Container Transportation

Chatham Towing is the exclusive contractor of Portable Storage, and we are willing and able to transport any large storage containers.

Speciality Equipment & Machinery Transportation

From industrial equipment to motorcycles, you name it, we can tow it.

Police Towing

Chatham Towing is the exclusive contractor of the Chatham-Kent Police Department.

Secure Compound

Should your vehicle be required to be kept at Chatham Towing, it will be stored in a secure compound.
